Project Context

Yoroll aimed to establish brand awareness across the global AI creator ecosystem. The challenge was to reach AI founders, indie builders, and early adopters through trusted creator networks and curated AI media channels.

Core Objective

Drive global brand visibility and product exploration through KOL-led content distribution on X and strategic placement in top-tier AI newsletters.

AI Newsletter Placement: Featured in Ben's Bites (100K+ subscribers), one of the most trusted AI newsletters for founders and builders.
